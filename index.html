About, philosophy, etc.

I have a varied background, and am on my fourth, probably fifth, act. I’m deeply curious about the world around me and try to learn about something new each day via: Subscribing to quality publications, having conversations with interesting people, teaching myself new skills, and always finding an excuse to travel.

Work

I hold degrees in engineering and finance, though I consider my university experience to be somewhat self-limiting creatively. That propelled me to launch an online fashion brand after graduation (2009) which in some ways was pre-ecommerce and pre-Instagram, for better or worse. Since, I’ve zigged and zagged through my career, spending time at companies like Uber and Aēsop, and consider myself firmly acquainted and rooted in the world of technology, specifically the branch that is, consumer internet.

Projects

I am currently working on a mindfulness-inspired personal care project formulated for athletes and enthusiasts alike. The brand, Minds and Recreation (launching 2020), is the culmination of research on skin hydration and the mind-body relationship and it's effects on skin health.

I recently spent time to (finally) find a new permanent home for my photos, essays, and general life updates, by creating a (Wordpress powered) weblog, Conscious Standard. I wrote a short post about this here.

Virtues

Earlier this year, inspired by a Benjamin Franklin productivity piece, I decided to write my own personal values to serve as my own life's navigation, to keep me grounded, and help me live with more intent. What began as an unnecessary lengthy list has now been condensed to set of five words: HumilityEmbody modesty and refrain or minimize from self-promotion, resistanceFight for what is right, be vocal for the people and things which matter to me, consciousnessEngage intentionally and with volition, colorBecome lost in the arts, study what makes the world beautiful, and compassionBring love and joy to people, self, and things. When I'm stuck or need a prescription of self-awareness, I turn to these.
