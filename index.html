About, philosophy, etc.

I have a varied background, and am on my fourth, probably fifth, act. I’m deeply curious about the world around me and try to learn about something new each day via: Subscribing to quality publications, having conversations with interesting people, teaching myself new skills, and always finding an opportunity to travel.

Work

I hold degrees in engineering and finance, though I considered my university experience self-limiting creatively as it propelled me to launch an online fashion brand upon graduation in 2009. In a way it was pre-ecommerce, and certainly pre-Instagram, for better or worse. Since, I’ve zigged and zagged through my career, spending time at companies like Uber and Aēsop, and consider myself firmly acquainted and rooted in the world of technology, specifically the branch that is, consumer internet.

Projects

I am currently working on a mindfulness-inspired personal care project aimed for athletes and enthusiasts alike. The brand, Minds and Recreation, is the culmination of research on skin hydration and the mind-body relationship and it's effects on skin health. The brand is launching 2020.

After witnessing platforms come and go, and assessing my own approach to social media consumption and presence, I’ve decided it’s important to share content (photos, essays, etc.) under a system I ultimately own and control. Despite a headless CMS being an ideal solution for me here, it’s probably not justified financially yet for personal usage. Thus I’ve elected to use Wordpress — a very customizable solution. Conscious Standard is my new weblog, and you can visit it from the header.

Virtues

Earlier this year, inspired by a Benjamin Franklin productivity piece, I decided to write my own personal values to serve as my own life's navigation, to keep me grounded, and help me live with more intent. What began as an unnecessary lengthy list has now been condensed to set of five words: HumilityEmbody modesty and refrain or minimize from self-promotion, resistanceFight for what is right, be vocal for the people and things which matter to me, consciousnessEngage intentionally and with volition, colorBecome lost in the arts, study what makes the world beautiful, and compassionBring love and joy to people, self, and things. When I'm stuck or need a prescription of self-awareness, I turn to these.
